The Most Dominant Athletes of the X-GAMES


Tony Hawk

Out of the 17 total X Games events that Tony Hawk entered, he walked away with 16 medals, 10 of which were gold. Hawk also claims one of most famous X Games
moments in history: The first skateboard 900, landed in 1999.


Shaun White

Shaun White has racked up 13 gold medals at the Winter X Games over his career. He also has two gold medals at the summer X Games.


Bob Burnquist

Burnquist retired with the record of the most medals won at the Summer X Games by an individual, with a total of 30 (14 gold, 8 silver and 8 bronze) and as the only person having competed in every single X Games summer event (his first being in 1995, every year through to 2017).

Travis Pastrana

In total, Travis Pastrana holds 17 medals (11 gold, four silver and two bronze). Among those medals, 13 are from Moto X, while the remaining four are from Rally Car. He is one of the most decorated Moto X athletes in X Games history and was the first athlete to have completed a double backflip on his motorcycle.

Dave Mirra

Mirra was an accomplished BMX rider and one of the most decorated athletes in ESPN's X Games history, medaling every year from 1995 to 2008 and winning 14 gold medals. His onetime record of 24 career medals was broken in 2013 by Bob Burnquist.
